Concept of P3T token
1. How do you make money?

Commissions from buy, sell and transfer movements of P3T tokens.

Commissions from your referral links.

Dividends from subsequent games built on the P3T tokens.

2. What is it «P3T Token»?

P3T is a decentralized infrastructure project created by a professional community in cryptography science, finance and investment-banking.

P3T creates an ecosystem of active crypto-investors based on the TRON blockchain, which can guarantee transparency, safety and liquidity.

P3T owners can get a return in P3T tokens, convertible to TRX when there are new platform users, new services implementation and partnership program’s cooperation.

3. Mathematical model

Commission fee for deposit: 10% of the deposit amount.

Fee for withdrawal: 4% from the amount.

Commission fee for transfer to another wallet: 1% from transferred amount.

Example - You have bought 10 000 P3T for 100 TRX, than 10 TRX will be proportionally distributed through all P3T owners.

33% of 10% charging fees sharing between users who has a referral link.

According to this model token pricing balancing by supply and demand from ecosystem’s users, herewith token is secured from manipulation.
