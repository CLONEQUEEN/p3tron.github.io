How it works?

There are 106 countries, all starting from 50 TRX. If you buy a country, to take over the country's ownership, the next player will need to pay 1.5x your price. Once a country ownership changes hand, you will receive 110% of your purchase price (one-time). 10% of the purchase price will go to P3T token owners as dividends. 5% goes to dev fee. 25% of the purchase price will go to the Last-Buyer Pot. If there is no purchase of any country in the next 24 hours, the last buyer take the Last-Buyer Pot home. Once there is a country purchase, the Last-Buyer Pot countdown resets to 24 hours.

FAQ

5% - Dev comission, 10% - P3T Token owners, 25% - Jackpot

Actually, nothing! Smart contract will be safe with all the balance. And it will continue to pay you every day. It works independent from anyone or anything.
